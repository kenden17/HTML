Vocabulary

    -Substinance Agricultre- farming to sustain oneself
    -Commercial Agricultre- farming to sell crops on the market
    -Plantations- largescale farming of a cash crop
    -Intensive Agricultre- using every bit of land to its maximum capacity
    -Pastoral Nomadism- moving in a set route with sheep or camels without a settled home
    -Slash and Burn- depleting an area of its resources then cutting down trees to farm on new land
    -Agribusiness- farming relating to business espeically with large corperations
    -Agriculture- the deliberate modification of the Earth's surface through cultivation of plants
    -Agricultural Revolution- the thime when humans first began domesticating animals and plants
    -Cereal Grain- grass that yields grain for food
    -Cereal- seed from a cereal grain
    -Food Security- physical, economic, and social access to safe food at all times
    -Transhumance- seasonal migration of livestock between mountains and lowland
    -Aquafaroming/ Aquaculture- cultivation of sea food under controlled conditions
    -Mixed Crop and Livstock- integration of crops and livestock

Substinance vs Commercial:

Agricultre is practiced in many different ways. This can vary depending on where you are and the economic level of your country. The two main types are:

    -Substinance Agricultre
    -Commercial Agriculture

Substinance Agricultre is farming to feed yoursef and/or your town. This is not to make profit. Some methods of farming can be intensive or non-intesnive, slash and burn, and pastoral nomadism.
Commercial Agricultre is farming to make a profit by selling crops on the market. Some methods of farming include intensive, and plantations.

Rice Farming

Rice Farming has many different ways that it is practiced. It can be substinance or intensive. It can be hard to distinguish the two because they are both common practice in LDC's. A common way to tell is the use of space. If it seems that there is a systematic way to farming, then it is likely intensive. But, on the other hand, substinance (non-intensive) rice farming often has wild rice patteis with no rows or order.

Plantations

Plantations are a type of intensive, commerical farming producing cash crops. These are typically found in LDC's or wherever large scale labor is cheap. So crops include, but are not limited to; cotton, rubber, coffee, coca beans, jute, sugarcane, and bananas.
