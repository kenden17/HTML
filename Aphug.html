Agriculture

Agricultre is practiced in many different ways. This can vary depending on where you are and the economic level of your country. The two main types are:

    -Substinance Agricultre
    -Commercial Agriculture

Substinance Agricultre is farming to feed yoursef and/or your town. This is not to make profit. Some methods of farming can be intensive or non-intesnive, slash and burn, and pastoral nomadism. Commercial Agricultre is farming to make a profit by selling crops on the market. Some methods of farming include intensive, and plantations.
