Biology

Chapter 1: Introduction to Biology

Biology is the study of life, and scientists use the scientific method to explore and understand the natural world.

Chapter 2: The Chemistry of Life

Life is built on chemistry, with atoms and molecules forming the basic building blocks of matter, and water's unique properties supporting life processes.

Chapter 3: Cell Structure and Function

Cells are the basic unit of life, with different structures like the nucleus, mitochondria, and cell membrane performing specific functions to maintain life.

Chapter 4: Energy and Metabolism

Living organisms need energy to grow, reproduce, and carry out life processes, and metabolism refers to the chemical reactions that occur within cells to convert energy.

Chapter 5: Cell Division

Cell division is essential for growth and reproduction, and processes like mitosis and meiosis ensure that cells replicate or form gametes with the correct number of chromosomes.

Chapter 6: Genetics

Genetics explores how traits are inherited through genes, with dominant and recessive alleles determining inherited characteristics through patterns of inheritance.

Chapter 7: Evolution

Evolution explains how species change over time through natural selection, where beneficial traits increase an organism's chances of survival and reproduction.
