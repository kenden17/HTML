Track 2025

Season Recap

The 2025 track season was a chance to learn and grow individually. As a freshman my goal was to make a permenant spot for me on the team for the years to come. And by winning freshman of the year as well as a varsity letter, I can say that I achieved my goal.

This season took everything I could give.

Highlights

  • Raced in All Varsity Meets
  • Fastest 100 Meters - 12.1 seconds
  • Top Ten Freshman Discus Throwers All Time

Next Season Goals

Explosiveness

Work on my starts and beat all competition in the first 3 steps.

Film

Rewatch film of sprints until my form is perfect.

Off Season

Sprinting will help me get to the next level in all sports, so off season training must be as rigorous as in season.

Growth for the Future

This season was decent for me, but I need to separate myself from the top sprinters in the conference. In the future they are only going to get faster, and that means I am going to have to outwork them every single day. The moment knows.
