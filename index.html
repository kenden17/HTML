Updates: This site is coded by students, so updates are regular to improve the user experience. v5.56

Donations: Keeping the website running costs money, and lots of time, and to keep this tool free we rely on donations. If you find this website useful, please consider a donation.

Content: Content on this site is what separates it from other competing study tools. There are relevant, and up-to-date notes for each class. Each was formatted by students who are currently in the class.

About Us: Peer Notes is an online resource developed by students to bring understandable and quality notes to others. It is updated every few days to keep the notes up to date. Please consider a donation to keep this resource running, as it does cost money. All classes have honors combined with the regular classes, the notes are similar.
