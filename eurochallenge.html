Euro Challenge

Season Recap

Euro Challenge is a nation wide competition amoung asipiring high school students based around the economics of the Euro Area. Each group of 4 students selects a country in the EU and creates a proposition to boost the economic standings if the respective country.

The competetion truly was a challenge, competing with seniors from some of the most prestigous high schools in the nation. Our Cedarburg team took 1st in the Midwest Region and advanced to the national competition in New York City.

Highlights

  • Leading an Academic Team to Success
  • Spending Countless Hours Chasing Perfection
  • Answering Questions from Economic Majors in Real Time
